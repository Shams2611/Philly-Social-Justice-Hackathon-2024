Your Rights and Support

Finding Abortion Funding

Abortion funds exist to cover the costs of your abortion care. In Philadelphia, minors who go through the judicial bypass process do not need to pay ANY money for the cost of their care. If you are able to cover any amount, whether that be $200, $50, or $5, the clinic will appreciate any contribution. However, you will NOT be turned away if you can’t pay. The clinics can work with you to apply for abortion funding. If you choose to not participate in the judicial bypass process and instead get your abortion in New Jersey, those clinics can assist you in securing funding but everything may not be covered.

Some funds require the patient themself call the fund hotline, and other funds require the clinic representative to complete the application. The resources below include two of the funds available to you.
